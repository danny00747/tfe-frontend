Driver & License Requirements

When you pick the car up, you'll need:

  • Passport or national ID card
  • Driving licence
  • Credit card

You must be at least 21 years old to rent this car. Collision Damage Waiver (CDW)

Security Deposit : 700,00 €

  • At pick-up, the main driver will leave a refundable security deposit of 700,00 € on their credit card. Cash and debit cards are not accepted. The counter staff will confirm how much this will be.

Damage Excess : 1331,00 €

  • If the car's bodywork gets damaged, you’re covered by the Collision Damage Waiver. This means that the most you'll pay towards repairs is the damage excess ( 1331,00 €).
  • This cover is only valid if you stick to the terms of the rental agreement. It doesn't cover other parts of the car (e.g. windows, wheels, interior or undercarriage), or charges (e.g. for towing or off-road time), or anything in the car (e.g. child seats, GPS devices or personal belongings).

Mileage 450 kilometres per rental

  • If you drive more than 450 kilometres during your rental, you'll pay between 0,16 € and 0,32 € for each additional kilometre when you drop your car off.
